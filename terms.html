Financial terminology

Briefly about translating financial terminology. Most common financial terms in Russian.

In order to translate documents such as financial statements, the linguist must have an in-depth understanding of the specific terms that are used. To retain consistency, it is also important that these terms are translated in the same way across different documents– the terminology should be kept the same.

Glossaries can be a great help in retaining consistency. This means that even if different translators are working on translations for the same client – consistency will be maintained and inaccuracies will be reduced, as specific terms are saved in one database. Bagiroff Translations is trying to assist you in sticking to the generic pattern.

Always make sure that your language service provider is able to meet your needs in terms of quality, cost, time and security.

Terminology translated into Russian

  • Accounts Payable: Счета к оплате/ Кредиторская задолженность
  • Accounts Receivable: Дебиторская задолженность
  • Asset: Актив
  • Balance Sheet: Баланс
  • Cash Flow: Движение денежной наличности
  • Fixed Asset: Основные средства
  • Income Statement: Отчет о доходах
  • Liability: Ответственность, обязятельство
  • Profit & Loss Statement: Отчет о прибылях и убытках
  • Annual Percentage Rate: Годовая процентная ставка
  • Collateral : Залог
  • Loan-to-Value: Стоимость кредита
  • Debt-Service Coverage Ratio: Коэффициент покрытия обслуживания долга
  • Lien: Залоговое право
  • Personal Guarantee : Личная гарантия
  • Financial Statements: Финансовая отчетность
  • Debt Consolidation: Консолидация долга
  • Gross Profit: Валовая прибыль
  • Statement of Cash Flow: Отчет о движении денежных средств
  • Credit Limit: Кредитный лимит
